What it does

Your outboard gear,
finally integrated.

Patchbay is the I/O insert plugin I needed for long time. It measures and compensates for your outboard's round-trip latency automatically, routes audio across different interfaces when cables are connected, and makes any piece of hardware fully dry/wet and M/S compatible — on top of many other things that were not possible before.

The Problem

Pro Tools' native I/O insert is... let's say limited. You can't even bypass it (you have to deactivate it) and you can't even pair I/O that are not close to each other AND not in odd/even order (1-2, 3-4 etc.).

The Solution

Patchbay can do so much more. Any input/output you want (why not Input 1 L / Input 14 R for example). With a 3 stages PING feature, it calculates your outboard latency and compensates for it in real time (the 3 PING avoids potential calculation error, to override ProTools hectic behavior), links send and return gain to drive into your gear (you can even automate that!), pre-emphasis/de-emphasis EQ to help your gear react more like you would want it to be. Polarity, phase aligment tool, M/S, you can even use Patchbay on your Masterbus and route it to your laptop speakers for a quick check without having to change ProTools I/O and wait for the session to reload.

Finally!

What does it do?

01

Automatic Latency Compensation

Measures round-trip latency of your outboard and compensates precisely with its 3 PING process to avoid any miscalculation.

02

Linked Send / Return

Drive into your hardware insert. You can even automate this, push it more in the choruses for example.

03

Dry / Wet + Mid / Side

Any outboard chain becomes parallel and mid/side compatible. And no more phase issues!

04

Cross-Interface Routing

Route audio to any input or output, even across different interfaces — as long as a cable is physically connected (ofc).

05

Pre-emphasis / De-emphasis EQ

Shape the signal before and after your hardware. Can sometimes save you from having to buy the sidechain high-pass filter version of your gear ;).

06

Phase Alignment & Polarity

Fine-tuned phase alignment tool and polarity switch in case of needs (useful when driving transformers for example).

07

Auto-Gain

A basic gain match function to avoid getting fooled by loudness and help gain staging when using dry/wet.

08

Customizable GUI

You can make it bigger, smaller, change the colors, the contrast, the brightness of eveything. You can also change the fonts. You can even choose different models of knobs. GUI is not my forte but at least everyone can pick its favorite ugly one :).
